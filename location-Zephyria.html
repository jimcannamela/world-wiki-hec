Zephyria

Zephyria is isolated from other regions, rarely visited by most. Its mines are filled with palladiumn crystals, the most sort after resources that keeps all machines functioning. The day of rekonning came with devasting consequences for Zephyria, laying bare all that were there.

Candace Chen, a millennial drone self-sequestered in a tower, is devoted to routine. Candace won't be able to make it on her own forever, though. Enter a group of survivors, led by the power-hungry IT tech Rich. They're trying to keep the Facility running, where, Rich promises, they will have everything they need to start society anew. But Candace is carrying a secret she knows Rich will exploit.
